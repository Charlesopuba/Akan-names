Akan Tribe names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture ,frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born ,
for instance if a male was born on a Sunday,the name they will be assigned is Kwasi,Monday Kwadwo,in females the ones born on Sunday will be Akosua,on Monday will be Adwoa.
A user can input the day and or date ,plus the year he /she was born and my app will generate their respective Akan name
